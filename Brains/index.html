About us

Brains of the future is the first open club in UKTC where you can join without any experiences.

We are small group of people who want to build our knowledges together and provides each self the opportunity to learn something different, something new and interesting in the field of technology.

Our mission is to organize workshops, lectures and competitions with ourselves help and ideas, to organize different field trips to the biggest IT companies and mostly to learn each other and have fun, too.
